Intro

developer & teacher

My name is Lennyroy Robles and I love to find elegant solutions to complex problems.I am fullstack web developer focusing on front-end JavaScript frameworks(React) and cutting edge Web Design(SASS, CSS Grid).

I am also a JavaScript teacher specializing in introductory coding courses serving 5th-9th grade. I personally believe that anyone that WANTS to learn coding, should have resources available to do so. Computer Science, and the economic opportunities it affords, are crucial in these uncertain times, and introducing more young women and P.O.C to coding is a lifelong passion of mine.

Get in touch @ lennyroyrobles@gmail.com

Web presence in 2019 is more important than ever, Let's get in touch and talk about your vision for your site!
